Photography

Around 6 years ago, I picked up photography as a hobbie. It's lovely going out and capturing places and things I like. I never really had a 'style' or preference if I say so myself, although I do like capturing landscapes.

As with many starting photographers these days, I started out with digital. I have a DSLR and that's what I picked up first, but I've been shooting more and more with my phone lately.

That's a photo of my town, 2022. 35mm film.

Later, I discovered film and I'm enjoying how manual and slower it is and how thoughtful you have to be when deciding what deserves a photo and what not. Nowadays I only use it for special ocasions or trips. It's cool to see maybe half a year(or more!) of your life condensed into only 36 photos.

Alongside film, I started out with astrophotography as well and I love it!!! It is obviously made with my DSLR and I think you should try it! The entry barrier is lower than you might think.
